# Altera Terra — Private World Website

## Brand Positioning
Altera Terra is not a travel agency. It is a sovereign realm of curated access — a house of refined journeys, cultivated living, aesthetic intelligence, and discreet privilege. The website must feel like being invited into a private world, not entering a service business.

## Design Philosophy
- **Expensive in taste, not in noise**
- Restraint, distinction, private-world immersion, editorial elegance
- Old European grand tour salon meets contemporary travel atelier
- Museum-grade minimalism with cultivated sensuality

## Color Palette (Mineral & Stone)
- Parchment: #F5F0E8 (warm ivory background)
- Chalk: #EDE8DF (secondary warm)
- Travertine: #D4CBC0 (stone accent)
- Charcoal: #2A2A2A (primary text)
- Deep Black: #1A1714 (hero overlays)
- Bronze: #9E8A6E (restrained metallic accent — NOT gold)
- Mist: #8A8278 (secondary text)

## Typography
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (serif, literary, architectural)
- Body: Inter (clean, quiet)
- Tracking: generous letter-spacing for uppercase labels

## Homepage Flow (Single Page — 7 Sections)
1. **Hero** — Full-screen cinematic palazzo hallway, logo crest, brand name in elegant serif, motto
2. **Overture** — Brand philosophy excerpt, poetic and literary
3. **The House** — What Altera Terra is — 5 service pillars with editorial imagery
4. **A Private World** — Full-bleed cinematic image with overlaid philosophical text
5. **Preferred Access** — Little Emperors relationship, restrained credibility layer
6. **Enquire** — Private enquiry invitation, application-style feel
7. **Footer** — Minimal, sovereign, with locations and legal
